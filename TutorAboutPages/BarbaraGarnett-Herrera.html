About Barbara Garnett-Herrera

Get to know your dedicated tutor!

Barbara Garnett-Herrera

Barbara has years of experience working with children academically. Her background includes seven years of substitute teaching in the Leander ISD. She has worked with almost every grade and spent a great deal of that time working in SPED. She is passionate about helping children succeed and doing so in a positive way. “Children fuel off our energy and mindset, so creating a happy environment and rewarding their strengths, builds confidence when overcoming challenges .” Barbara has seven daughters (practically a football team), including her last two at home, 15 year old twins. She enjoys family vacations and going to football games with her amazing husband. After two years in college she started writing for Text-Broker and Writer’s Digest and is currently working on writing her own book and dreams of writing a series of children’s books.
