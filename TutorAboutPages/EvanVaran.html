About Evan Varan

Evan Varan

Hello! I'm Evan Varan, and I tutor all sorts of things. I am a college graduate from Texas State University with a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering. I also have a minor in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I have been tutoring virtually and in person for over 8 years. I also have experience teaching students with special needs. I currently offer tutoring in Math & Science for Kindergarten through College. I can also tutor in chemistry, physics, computer science, and more. Hope to see you soon!

When I'm not teaching, I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, guitar, soccer, and sand volleyball.

Evan's Values

Empathy

I take the time to understand my students' unique experiences, challenges, and needs. By fostering a supportive and compassionate environment, I help them feel seen, valued, and capable of achieving their goals.

Growth

I am committed to continuous learning and self-improvement, both for myself and my students. I strive to inspire a love of learning, encouraging my students to embrace challenges and realize their full potential.

Integrity

As a teacher, I model honesty and ethical behavior in every aspect of my work. I believe that trust is the foundation of effective teaching, and I ensure that my actions align with the values I teach my students.
