About Myan Nelson

Myan Nelson

Greetings everyone! My name is Myan Nelson, and I would love to be your child's math tutor. Currently, I specialize in tutoring 1st-7th grade Math. I am a certified teacher and licensed math interventionist who taught 5th and 6th grade Math for a total of 8 years. I was also a 3rd grade teacher for two years. I had the privilege of staying home with my son during his elementary school years and subbed for several years at his elementary before returning to full-time teaching. My teaching goal now is to not only assist students one on one to grow in their math skills but to also emotionally guide them through any anxieties or fears that they might have towards math. Growing up with academic anxieties myself, I can empathize with these feelings. Providing that safe place to make Math mistakes (a necessity for Math growth) is my mission. I look forward to working with your child!
