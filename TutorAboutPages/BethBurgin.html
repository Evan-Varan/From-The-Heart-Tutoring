About Beth Burgin

Get to know your dedicated tutor!

Beth Burgin

Hi! I'm Beth Burgin, Co-owner and tutor for From the Heart Tutoring. I am a certified teacher. I have worked as a classroom teacher in public schools for over nine years. I have experience with Dyslexia, Dysgraphia, ESL, ADHD, and Autism. I specialize in 6th grade Math, 7th grade Math, 8th grade Math and Algebra. Prior to teaching I worked as an engineer in Austin, TX. My degree is in Engineering from the University of TX at Austin.

When I'm not teaching, I enjoy hanging out with my dog Benji, watching disaster movies, and gardening.
