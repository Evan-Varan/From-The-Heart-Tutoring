Taylor Thomas

Hi, I'm Taylor Thomas! I'm a biology graduate and current master's student in Instructional Design and Learning Technology at UTA. I've spent over three years teaching and developing curriculum for science grades 7-12, and I love helping students tackle tough concepts and feel more confident in their learning. Whether it's prepping for a test or just getting through a tricky homework assignment, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

Outside of tutoring, I'm usually reading, traveling, or working on creative projects and I'm always looking for new ways to make learning fun and engaging. I'm excited to meet you!
