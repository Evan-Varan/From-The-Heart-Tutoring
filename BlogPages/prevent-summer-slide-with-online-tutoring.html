Prevent the Summer Slide with Online Tutoring

Updated April 2025
Evan Varan

Summer break is a well-deserved time for students to relax, recharge, and enjoy time with family. But without academic engagement, many students experience a learning loss known as the “summer slide.” This can make it harder to start the new school year with confidence. Online tutoring offers a flexible and effective way to keep learning fresh without sacrificing summer fun.

What Is the Summer Slide?

The summer slide refers to the loss of academic skills that occurs when students are out of school for extended periods. Studies show that students can lose up to two months of math and reading progress during the summer. These setbacks add up over the years, especially in early grades or for students already struggling.

Who Is Most Affected?

All students are at risk, but the summer slide most significantly impacts:

  • Elementary school students building foundational skills
  • Students with learning gaps or special learning needs
  • Students who lack access to enrichment activities

Why Online Tutoring Is an Ideal Summer Solution

1. Flexible Scheduling

Online sessions can be scheduled around vacations, camps, and family time. Even 1–2 sessions a week can make a meaningful impact on retention and growth.

2. Personalized, Targeted Review

Online tutors identify each student’s specific strengths and areas of need. Summer sessions are tailored to reinforce prior concepts or preview next year’s material.

3. Build Confidence Before the School Year

Starting the school year strong can set the tone for long-term success. Students who review over the summer often feel more prepared and motivated in the fall.

4. Keep the Mind Active

Summer learning doesn’t need to feel like school. Online tutoring sessions can include games, interactive problem-solving, and creative learning that keeps students engaged and thinking critically.

Subjects to Focus On Over the Summer

Summer tutoring can focus on:

  • Reading comprehension and vocabulary development
  • Math fluency and foundational skills
  • Writing structure and expression
  • Study habits and organization
  • Preparation for standardized tests or entrance exams

Make This Summer Count

You don’t have to choose between fun and learning. With online tutoring, students can enjoy their break while keeping their academic progress on track.

Schedule a summer session today and set your child up for a confident, successful return to school this fall.
