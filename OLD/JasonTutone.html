About Jason Tutone

Get to know your dedicated tutor!

Jason Tutone

Hi!  My name is Jason Tutone, and I am a Gifted and Talented teacher at River Place Elementary in LISD.   I have vast teaching experience in upper elementary and extensive tutoring experience with all grade levels.   I have an undergraduate degree in Journalism and a Masters in Elementary Education.  I make great connections with all my students.  I have worked with students from varying backgrounds and have been successful with students who have ADHD and Autism. I look forward to making a connection with your child and helping them succeed in their academics!
